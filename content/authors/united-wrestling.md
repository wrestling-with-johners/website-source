Professional Wrestling promotion founded in 2019 and based in Oxford, Oxfordshire.
We pride ourselves on being all inclusive! Labels mean nothing when you step through a United door. It is all about the best story telling, with the best wrestling featuring the very best the UK has to offer. While providing a platform for newer talent to showcase what they can do. We run out of home at the Tap Social Movement's Taproom in North Hinksey.
